Are your quotes really transparent?
Absolutely. Our clients frequently praise us for being "fairly priced unlike others." We provide detailed, upfront quotes before any work begins, ensuring there are zero hidden fees or surprises.
How quickly can you complete an installation?
We pride ourselves on rapid turnaround. It's not uncommon for us to quote a job on Friday and have it completely finished by Monday at 9 AM. We arrive on time with a fully equipped crew to get the job done efficiently.
Can you handle heavy-duty residential wiring?
Yes. We specialize in custom setups, including running 220 lines across properties (e.g., house to garage for welders), installing 20 amp circuits, upgrading breaker boxes, and comprehensive Generac home generator installations.

Yes, Grounded Electrical Company is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We bring premium, code-compliant craftsmanship to every single job to protect your home and investment.
